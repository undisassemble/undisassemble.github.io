Yet Another Packer

A free and open source protector for 64-bit Windows PEs.

Features

Despite its name, YAP does more than just packing. YAP can fully disassemble your application and apply the same mutation used for the packer, so that even if your application gets unpacked, reverse engineers will have a hell of a time trying to figure out how your app works.

Packer

  • Dynamic shellcode generation & mutation
  • Import obfuscation & emulation
  • Anti-debug
  • Immitation
  • SDK
  • And more!

Reassembler

  • Symbol stripping
  • Mutation
  • SDK integration
